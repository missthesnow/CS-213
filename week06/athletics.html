Athletics

The Themis Myrmidons are the athletic teams of Themis University. Though the athletic program mainly focuses on track and field events, the school sponsors a numerous variety of varsity sports. The school has won several international championships, and has had many alumni go on to compete in the Olympics. In true Greek athletic tradition, Themis University hosts an annual decathlon, open to students, the community, and professional and amateur athletes alike.

Themidan Decathlon

The Themidan Decathlon is an annual, university hosted event consisting of a series of ten combined track and field athletic events. The event is held over two consecutive days and the winners are determined by the combined performance in all.

After providing an athletic physical, anyone can compete and performance is judged on a points system in each event, not by the position achieved.

The purpose of the decathlon is to foster a love of athletic excellent in all who choose to participate.
