Art

At Themis University, we believe art is a diverse range of human activities which express the intrinsic imagination, conceptual ideas, or technical skill. It is only through careful study that a student may learn to fully appreciate the divine order inherent within artistic expression.
While studying under masterful professors, art students at Themis University create incredible works of art, hailed worldwide for their beauty and emotional power.
We believe there is no concrete definition of what constitutes art and the true nature therein lies in the ever-changing ideas, descriptions and practices of imaginative or technical artistic skill.

All students at the university, regardless of academic discipline, develop valuable humanities skils, including the appreciative criticism and history of art.

Academic Offerings

The university offers undergraduate courses in the three classical branches of visual art, to include painting, sculpture and architecture. In addition, elective courses in performing arts, including Music, theatre, film, and dance are available for a broader course of study.
For the truly applied artist, the Themis College of Arts offers a graduate program that confers MFAs in Graphic Design, Painting/Printmaking, Photography, and Sculpture.
